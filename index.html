Biography

Jiubel is a rapper and singer from Miami who started his musical career in

2017 under the name Jaebanzz. He gained popularity with his songs like “real love?”

and “Astray”, which featured his melodic and emotional style.


He signed with FADER Label, an independent record label in New York City, in 2020 and

his stage name to Jiubel. He released his first single under the label, “Willow’s World”, which

was a tribute to his late friend and fellow rapper, Willow.


He took a break from music for a year and came back in 2021 with his single, “Runnin”, which

his desire to escape from the troubles of the world. He released more singles in 2022, such as “Make

A Lover Mine”, “Castaway”, and “Rebirth”, which showed his versatility and growth as an artist.

Jiubel is known for his catchy hooks, smooth vocals, and honest lyrics, which resonate with his fans.

He is also active on social media platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube, where he interacts with

his followers and shares his music videos.


Jiubel is one of the most promising and exciting talents in the rap scene, and he is expected to release his

album soon. He has been praised by critics and peers for his originality and creativity. He is inspired by

artists like Drake, Post Malone, and The Weeknd.


He hopes to inspire others with his music and message.
